Beeroscope Skeleton Website

About Us

The Beeroscope website is composed of two projects designed to help users find their beer preferences.

The first project is a direct web search recommender customized so that the users will be able to search the type of beers that they would like to try out.

The second project is a horoscope recommending users what type of beers they would like to buy based on their birth month.

Project 2

For Project 2, we will be building a horoscope based on the user's birth month. We will program a horoscope based on hospitality industry advice regarding the best times to serve beer.

Spring Season

The Spring Season is ideal for the following types of beers: dry, saisons, common, fresh hops, bocks, pine.

Summer Season

The Summer Season is ideal for the following types of beers: fruit, wheat, pale and hoppy, classic pilsner, hazy IPA, sour.

Fall Season

The Fall Season is ideal for the following types of beers: pumpkin, maple, amber, belgian, oktoberfest.

Winter Season

The Winter Season is ideal for the following types of beers: stouts, porters, imperial, bourbon, chocolate and coffee, eggnog.
